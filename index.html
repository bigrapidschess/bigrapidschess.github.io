Welcome

This is currently an informal gathering of chess players and those that are interested in learning more about chess. We generally meet twice each week:

We gather on campus at Ferris State University in the University Center near the Starbucks on Wednesdays from 2:00 to 4:00 pm.

We also meet at the Big Rapids Community Library in the Community room on Thursdays from 5:00 to 7:00 pm. Anyone interested in learning, studying, or playing chess is invited to attend both of these. You can learn more about our group on our About us page. Due to the response to the COVID-19 outbreak, our group has been on a prolonged hiatus. We are working on resuming regular meetings again this summer (2021) as things become more normal once again. Thanks for your patience.
